COINCUBE | Marketplace

  • KYC option via Meld/Mavapay — direct fiat on-ramp / off-ramp, transact within the app.

Bitcoin is permissionless money. You shouldn't need permission to buy or sell it. The Marketplace eliminates the friction of moving funds between unsafe exchanges and complex wallets — it's all under one roof.

Transact

COINCUBE | Liquid

  • Real-world utility — move funds to the Liquid Wallet for instant, near-zero-fee payments.
  • Lightning Network and Liquid Network support.
  • Pay merchants or send to friends globally without touching deep cold storage.

The Liquid wallet facilitates daily utility through immediate transactions, providing frictionless access to funds for daily spending.

Transport

COINCUBE | Vault

  • Advanced multi-sig Bitcoin wallet using Taproot and Miniscript.
  • Create additional spend paths for inheritance and recovery.
  • Time-lock funds for forced hold and security. (Coming Soon)

The Vault offers smart custody — manage wealth securely with inheritance recovery and time-locked savings. Peace of mind for the future.

Transport

COINCUBE | Connect

  • Easily back up your wallet descriptor with our Connect service.
  • Run your own Bitcoin node — or use our Esplora service free with Connect.
  • Enable Duress Mode — have a wrench attack plan already in place.
  • Powers Legacy — our inheritance and recovery protocol. Connect is the backbone that makes time-locked recovery and family key onboarding possible.

Our Connect service is the layer that lifts the Vault into a new class of self-custodial wallets — providing backup, node access, duress protection, and the infrastructure behind our Legacy inheritance protocol.

Transport

COINCUBE | Keychain

  • Turn any phone into a Bitcoin signing device.

  • Works seamlessly with the Vault.

Our companion mobile app acts as your dedicated signing device — simple inheritance, strong security, fewer hardware devices.
